Price Analytics

Price analytics in Oracle CPQ involves leveraging historical data, AI-powered models, and real-time guidance to help sales teams optimize pricing, increase profitability, and close deals faster. Shopping Cart

Shopping cart is a virtual tool that allows users to view, manage, and hold products and services for purchase before creating a quote or order. It displays the number of items, individual prices, and the total cost, enabling users to build complex configurations and then either checkout items to a new quote or add them to an existing one. It integrates with the CPQ configuration and commerce sections to provide a seamless quote-to-cash experience
